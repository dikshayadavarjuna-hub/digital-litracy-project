Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist 
* Do not share details like OTPs, passwords or UPI PINs with anyone.
* Always check calls, messages or emails before you respond to them.
* Be careful when you click on links or download attachments that you do 
  not know.
* Use different passwords for each of your accounts.
* Try to use two-factor authentication whenever you can.
* Do not accept payment requests, from people you do not know on UPI 
  apps.
* Never enter your UPI PIN to get money.
* Keep your devices updated with the security updates.
* Log out of your accounts when you use computers or devices shared with 
  others.
* Report any cybercrime away through official channels.

Reporting: 
* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline Number: 1930
